Frequently Asked Questions

Where is my data stored?

Your account and organization related data is currently stored in Nürnberg, Falkenstein, Frankfurt (Germany) and Helsinki (Finland) under European privacy law. For "Expert" users payment information are processed by Stripe, Inc. in the United States.

What does "Fair Pricing" mean?

Organizations on the "Expert" plan are billed per active user, meaning those who log in at least once in a 30-day period. You receive a credit for inactive users, which will be applied to new or existing users in the next billing cycle.

What are the payment options?

We currently accept all major credit cards (e.g. Visa, MasterCard, American Express). Depending on your country you may also use services like AliPay, Giropay, iDEAL, SEPA or SOFORT.

What personal information do you ask for?

During registration we ask for your email address, full name and an account password. No additional information is required to sign up.
